This is a list of programs **currently** and **soon to be broadcast** by Xbox
Live; most are viewed via IPTV or video on demand applications (such as
sporting events via the ESPN application), while others, such as _Majors
Minute_ , are streamed by Xbox Live. However, most events are not available in
all regions.

Xbox Live simulcasts the ATP Masters 1000 tournaments in Indian Wells, Miami,
Toronto/Montreal and Cincinnati.

On May 2, 2012, it was announced that Inside Xbox would be discontinued to
focus more on its entertainment features.[1]
