**Kyochon F &B Co., Ltd.** (Korean: 교촌; Hanja: 橋村) is a South Korean fried
chicken restaurant chain. Founded in 1991,[1] Kyochon is one of the largest
Korean fried chicken restaurants in South Korea.[2] Kyochon operates some
restaurants in the United States as well. Kyochon has its head office in Osan,
Gyeongi-do.[3] In 2015, Kyochon opened its world largest outlet in Malaysia at
Pavilion KL. Kyochon had over 50 outlets nationwide.
