**Jiang Guangtao** (simplified Chinese: 姜广涛; traditional Chinese: 姜廣濤; pinyin:
_Jiāng Guǎngtāo_ ) is a Chinese actor, voice actor and dubbing director of
Mainland China, dubbing and directing foreign content in the Mandarin Chinese
format. Most of the roles that Jiang dubs for foreign films are considered
protagonist or secondary roles.
