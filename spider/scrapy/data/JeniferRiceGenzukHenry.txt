**Jenifer Rice-Genzuk Henry** , born as **Jenifer Sara Rice-Genzuk** and
previously known as **Jeni G.** , is an American screenwriter and former
singer. She is currently working as Executive Producer and co-showrunner on
the Freeform series _Grown-ish_. Before her time as a screenwriter, she was a
member of the American girl group Before Dark. She also served as a staff
writer on MTV's _Death Valley_ , an executive producer on BET's _The Game_ and
as a co-executive producer on the ABC series _Black-ish_ .

In 2001, Rice-Genzuk Henry graduated from USC Thornton School of Music with a
B.S. in Music Industry.[2]
