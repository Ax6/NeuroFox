**Kapatid Channel** (stylized as **Kapatid** ) is a Philippine pay television
channel owned by multimedia conglomerate TV5 Network. The channel offers
programming highlights from the Philippine television channels 5, 5 Plus,
Sari-Sari Channel, Colours, One News, One Sports, One PH, PBA Rush and INCTV.

Since 2011, the channel has broadcast local dramas, variety shows, game shows,
comedy programs and other contents from 5. Today, this channel has broadcast
sports, news & public affair programs, general entertainment & religious
programs from 5 Plus, One News, One Sports, PBA Rush, Sari-Sari Channel &
INCTV.
