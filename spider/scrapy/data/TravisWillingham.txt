**Travis Hampton Willingham** is an American voice actor who provides voices
for English-language versions of anime, animation and video games. He rose to
fame in the voice acting world for his portrayal of Roy Mustang in _Fullmetal
Alchemist_[1] and reprised the role in _Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood_. His
roles include Ginko in _Mushishi_ , Cleo in _Glass Fleet_ , Yu Kanda in
_D.Gray-man_ , Portgas D. Ace in the Funimation re-dub of _One Piece_ and
Takashi Morinozuka in _Ouran High School Host Club_. He also voices Thor in
several Marvel projects and King Roland II in Disney Junior's _Sofia the
First_. Between 2010 and 2018, he was the voice of Knuckles the Echidna from
the _Sonic the Hedgehog_ series. He is a cast member of _Critical Role_ , a
web series in which he and fellow voice actors participate in _Dungeons&
Dragons_.[2]

Willingham married fellow voice actor Laura Bailey on September 25, 2011.[3]
They reside in Los Angeles. He also participates in triathlons.[4] Their son,
Ronin Willingham, was born on June 28th, 2018.[5]

Willingham also has a brother named Carson.[6][7]
