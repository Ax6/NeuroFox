The **2018MTV Movie & TV Awards** was held on June 16, 2018, from the Barker
Hangar in Santa Monica, California, U.S. and was broadcast on June 18, 2018.
Similar to the 2016 MTV Movie Awards, the event was not aired live and was
pre-recorded on June 16 prior to its June 18 broadcast date. It was the 27th
edition of the awards and the second to jointly honor movies and television.
The ceremony was hosted by Tiffany Haddish.[1]

MTV announced the first few performers on May 23, 2018.[2]

The first few presenters were revealed on May 23, 2018 with the performers.[2]

The full list of nominees was announced on May 3, 2018.[3] Winners are listed
first, in **bold**.[4]
