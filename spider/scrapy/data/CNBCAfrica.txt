**CNBC Africa** is an African pay television network launched on 1 June 2007
and owned by CNBC and Africa Business News.[1] It is headquartered in Sandton,
Johannesburg.

The channel features programmes and updates from CNBC Europe, the Middle East,
India, Pakistan, the US and other CNBC affiliates around the world.
