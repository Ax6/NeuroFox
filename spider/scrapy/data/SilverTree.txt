**Silver Tree** is a film producer, director and writer originally from
Petaluma, California.[1][2] Silver Tree was the Directing Producer and
Executive Producer of the US television series _Suits_ and currently holds the
same position for the Netflix series _You_. She is also an episodic director
with dramatic, comedic and action credits including _Atypical_ , _Shameless_ ,
_The Mick_ , _A Million Little Things_ and _Lethal Weapon_.
