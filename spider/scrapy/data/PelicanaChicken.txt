**Pelicana Chicken** (Korean: 페리카나 Chicken) is a Korean fried chicken chain.
Its headquarters are in Gyeryong, South Chungcheong Province, South Korea.[1]
As of 2014, Yang Hee-Kweon is the CEO.[2][3] As of 2013, it had over 2,000
locations in South Korea and also has locations outside of South Korea.
Pelicana Chicken Malaysia held its "soft opening" on January 4, 2013, and the
company planned to hold the grand opening of the first full store in July
2013.[4] The chain expanded to the United States in 2014, and has established
locations in New York, New Jersey, Pennsylvania and Virginia. Pelicana has
also opened in Mainland China.
