The **50thNAACP Image Awards** ceremony, presented by the NAACP, honored
outstanding representations and achievements of people of color in motion
pictures, television, music, and literature during the 2018 calendar year. The
ceremony took place on March 30, 2019 at the Dolby Theatre, was hosted by
Anthony Anderson and broadcast on TV One.[1]
