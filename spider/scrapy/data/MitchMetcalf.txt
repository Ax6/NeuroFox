**Mitch Metcalf** is a television analyst and former scheduling executive for
NBC. Before the network promoted him in 2005, Metcalf was previously senior
vice president of program research for the network since 1999. He left the
company in 2011.[1][2] Later that year, he cofounded _Showbuzz Daily_ —a
website dedicated to box office and television analysis—with Mitch
Salem.[3][4]
