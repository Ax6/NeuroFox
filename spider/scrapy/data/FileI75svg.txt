600 mm by 600 mm (24 in by 24 in) Interstate shield, made to the
specifications of the 2004 edition of Standard Highway Signs (sign M1-1). Uses
the Roadgeek 2005 fonts. (United States law does not permit the copyrighting
of typeface designs, and the fonts are meant to be copies of a U.S.
Government-produced work anyway.) Colors are from [1] (Pantone Red 187 and
Blue 294), converted to RGB by [2]. The outside border has a width of 1 (1 mm)
and a color of black so it shows up; in reality, signs have no outside border.
